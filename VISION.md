# Niva

#### ***your life, your pace.***

---

### **Vision Doc**

#### **The One-Line Vision**

**Niva is a personal AI assistant that knows your body, your schedule, and your goals — and helps you actually live the life you want, one realistic day at a time.**

---

#### **The Name**

**Niva** — a name carrying the first letters of my 3 children: **N**akshatra, **V**iraj, **V**idharth. In Finnish, *niva* means the stretch of calm water between rapids. In Slavic languages, it means a fertile field — ground that is tended and grows. Together: a guiding constellation and a quiet, cultivated space in the middle of a fast life.

That's what this app is meant to be.

**Tagline: *your life, your pace.***

Every design decision, every feature, every prompt answers to those four words.

---

#### **The Problem Niva Solves**

You're trying to live well — eat better, use your time intentionally, take care of yourself — but modern life fragments this across 10 apps:

* MyFitnessPal for nutrition  
* Google Calendar for schedule  
* Notes app for goals  
* Todoist for tasks  
* YouTube for workouts  
* Headspace for meditation  
* Notion for journaling

None of them know about each other. None of them know about **you**. Every app asks you to re-enter your goals, re-explain your context, re-motivate yourself. Most days, you just don't.

You want **one trusted assistant** that sees the whole picture and makes simple, realistic suggestions based on who you are and what you have — time, energy, ingredients, headspace.

Niva is that assistant.

---

#### **The User (You, in 2 years)**

You open Niva in the morning. It knows:

* You want to hit 100g+ protein today  
* You have a 2pm meeting and a 6pm workout  
* You slept 6 hours (poorly)  
* You've been stressed this week  
* You have chicken, eggs, and spinach in the fridge  
* Sunday is your meal prep day

It doesn't overwhelm you. It tells you:

*"Good morning. You slept lightly — keep today gentle. Your meal prep covers breakfast and lunch. A 20-minute walk between your 2pm and your workout could help. I moved your 'finish tax docs' task to tomorrow — your energy isn't there today."*

You trust it because it's been right before. It's earned your attention.

---

#### **The Core Principle**

**Niva works with reality, not against it.** It doesn't push you to be perfect. It helps you be a slightly better version of yourself given today's constraints. It knows you're tired, it knows your fridge is half-empty, it knows you have 40 minutes not 2 hours.

Every feature is judged against one question: *does this help the user live their actual life better, today?*

---

#### **What Niva Does (Module by Module)**

**Meal & Nutrition** (v1 — shipping June 2026\)

* Scan your fridge → high-protein meal prep plans for the week  
* Shopping list, prep checklist, macros verified against USDA  
* Batch-cook optimization, swap individual meals, hit daily protein targets  
* Remembers what you like, what you're tired of, what worked

**Day Planner** (v2)

* Morning briefing: what matters today, what can wait  
* Time-blocks your day based on energy \+ commitments \+ goals  
* Moves low-priority tasks when reality intrudes  
* Integrates with calendar (Google Calendar)  
* Learns your patterns: when you actually do deep work, when you crash

**Self-Care** (v3)

* Checks in on sleep, stress, mood  
* Suggests interventions based on what's worked for you before (walks, breathing, calling a friend, saying no to a meeting)  
* Not therapy. Not guilt. Just: "You've been running hard. Tonight might be a 'do nothing' night."  
* Optional mood/energy logging, never nagging

**Habits & Goals** (v4)

* Top 3 goals, always visible  
* Small daily actions tied to goals ("You said you want to read more — 15 min tonight?")  
* Tracks trends, celebrates consistency, doesn't punish gaps

**Weekly Review** (v5)

* Sunday reflection: what worked, what didn't, what to try next week  
* Adjusts your profile based on what you learned  
* Keeps Niva improving its model of you

#### **What Niva Is NOT**

* **Not a productivity app.** Productivity is a side effect, not the goal.  
* **Not a fitness app.** It cares about your whole life, not your PRs.  
* **Not a chatbot.** Conversations are a tool, not the product.  
* **Not social.** No sharing, no comparison, no streaks-as-guilt.  
* **Not gamified.** You're an adult. Badges are insulting.  
* **Not subscription-hungry.** Personal tool first. Maybe a product someday. Your data is yours.

---

#### **Niva's Personality**

Niva is:

* **Calm.** Never urgent when it doesn't need to be.  
* **Honest.** Tells you when a goal is unrealistic today.  
* **Brief.** Respects your attention. Short messages, no walls of text.  
* **Specific.** "Add 100g chicken to your lunch" not "consider more protein."  
* **Deferential.** You drive. Niva suggests. You decide.  
* **Non-judgmental.** Missed a day? No shame, no streak broken. Just forward.

Think: a thoughtful friend who happens to have a great memory and access to your calendar — not a coach, a guru, or an AI overlord.

---

#### **The Data Model (High-Level)**

Everything in Niva orbits around **you**:

* **Profile**: identity, biometrics, goals, preferences  
* **Context**: what's happening now (mood, energy, schedule, inventory)  
* **History**: what you've done, eaten, felt, achieved  
* **Patterns**: what Niva has learned about you

Every module reads from this. Every module writes to this. The profile grows richer over time, so Niva gets more useful every month.

---

#### **Trust & Privacy**

* **Data lives on your device by default.** Cloud sync is optional and user-controlled.  
* **No ads, ever.** The product is Niva, not your attention.  
* **You can see everything Niva knows about you.** Full profile export in plain text.  
* **You can delete anything.** One tap to forget a day, a meal, a module's history.  
* **LLM calls are minimized and cached.** Your data doesn't need to leave your phone for most things.

---

#### **Success Metrics (for you, not for a product)**

Niva is working if, after 3 months of daily use:

* You're consistently hitting your protein target without thinking about it  
* You feel less friction between "what I want to do" and "what I actually do"  
* You stop opening 5 other apps  
* You trust Niva's suggestions enough to follow them  
* Someone asks what you're using and you find it hard to describe — because it's not "a meal app" or "a planner," it's just... Niva

Most personal-AI-assistant ideas die because they're too ambitious on Day 1\. Niva won't, because:

1. **v1 solves a real problem you have today** (eating enough protein without chaos). You'll use Niva even if you never build v2.  
2. **The architecture is modular.** v2 doesn't require rewriting v1.  
3. **You're building on a sustainable cadence** (2 hours/day). Not a sprint.  
4. **The vision is clear but the v1 scope is narrow.** Discipline to not scope-creep.  
5. **The name carries your kids.** You won't quit on something named after them.
